About Garden of Yggdrasil

Tyler Shelton began gardening when he was just a wee lad. His parents could tell it was going to be his calling in life. The first plant he planted was a potato. From then on all he cared about was gardening. Every year his garden, just like his dreams, got a little bit bigger. Then one day he had saved up enough money and decided to buy a garden store and create Garden of Yggdrasil.
